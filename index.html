syslog-protocol support in rsyslog

Rsyslog provides a trial implementation of the proposed syslog-protocol standard. The intention of this implementation is to find out what inside syslog-protocol is causing problems during implementation. As syslog-protocol is a standard under development, its support in rsyslog is highly volatile. It may change from release to release.
